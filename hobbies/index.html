I enjoy cross-stitch, crocheting, and knitting as relaxing activities during those rare moments I have to sit and relax. I like to find patterns or have patterns made from phots. Here are some examples of my work.

Additionally, I love to read who-done-it type crime and mystery novels. One of my favorite authors is Patricia Cornwell. Her Kay Scarpetta series has had me intrigued for over 20 years. I also very much enjoy the Kinsey Millhone series (an alphabet based series starting with “A is for Alibi”) by Sue Grafton. I’ve yet to complete either series but am in progress. Other authors I favor are Robin Cook, James Patterson, Tess Gerritsen, and Stephen King.

Something else I find enjoyment in, when I have the time, is baking and cooking. I do like trying new recipes and altering them until they work best for me. With baking I tend to gear more towards chocolate recipes but I have tried others. Some yummy items can be seen in the photos below. When I do cook, I do it from scratch, no box mixes in my house. I also like to make my own BBQ sauce. I do not use a recipe for that so it comes out a bit different every time, which is nice for less monotony. Cooking/Baking for me is a nice way to relax, enjoy good food, and please my family and friends.
